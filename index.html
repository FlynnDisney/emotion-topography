Emotions modelled as attractor basins in a valence × arousal state space. The three control parameters — confidence (precision weighting), surprise (prediction error), and goal momentum (free energy gradient) — reshape which emotional states are accessible. Based on dynamical systems approaches to emotion (Colombetti 2014; Lewis & Liu 2011) and predictive processing frameworks.
